ChatGPT for Legacy iOS?!

Yes, Indeed. Soon with the release of ChatGPT for Legacy iOS you will be able to use your favorite, most capable AI on your legacy iOS devices aswell.

ChatGPT for Legacy iOS supports most things that you already know from modern chatbot apps, such as thorough text conversations with context, image analysis by ChatGPT and image generation. Your conversations are also always saved automatically and can always be returned to whenever you want to.

Throughout the coming days ChatGPT for Legacy iOS will launch for iOS 6.0+, supporting iOS 7 and later aswell.

Discord Classic

Discord Classic is the only Discord client available for iOS 5 and 6. It has a beautiful and professional user interface and has a lot of features. More about it here.

BlueTweety

BlueTweety adds native BlueSky integration to iOS 6 and replaces Twitter's use completely. You may also use the bluesky-bridge when using BlueTweety. Available on SkyGlow and bag.xml!

iTunesStoreX

iTunesStoreX makes the iTunes Stores work again on legacy iOS. Simply install, then re-boot your devices and you can re-enjoy the stores with a fixed layout! Available on SkyGlow and bag.xml!

The Developer Central

The time has come. The Developer Central has been released.

The Developer Central is a hub for developers or anyone who are interested in starting development targeting old versions of iOS, offering guides, knowledge base articles, dev-blogs and more to help you become more familiar with Xcode and the programming language you need to use for iOS.

Popular community projects also have a chance of being featured on the Developer Central, aswell as your own blog posts, guides, and knowledge base articles. Simply submit them and we'll check them and evaluate them.
